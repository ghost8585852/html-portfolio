List of top 5 amazing movies

you must watch all of these :
  1. Maze Runner :

    Maze Runner follows a group of teenagers who wake up in a mysterious, ever-changing maze with no memory of their past lives. They must work together to navigate the maze, uncover its secrets, and survive while being pursued by terrifying creatures. As they search for a way out, they discover that they are part of a larger, sinister experiment.

  2. Batman(2022) :

    In The Batman (2022), Robert Pattinson portrays a darker, grittier version of Bruce Wayne, still in the early years of his crime-fighting career. As Batman, he investigates a series of murders by the Riddler, uncovering a deep web of corruption tied to Gotham's elite. The film explores Batman's struggle with his identity while forming key alliances with characters like Catwoman and the Penguin.

  3. Forrest gump(1994):

    Forrest Gump follows the extraordinary life of Forrest, a man with a low IQ but a kind heart, who unintentionally influences major historical events in the 20th century. Despite facing personal challenges, including his love for Jenny, he excels in everything from running to ping pong. The film explores themes of destiny, love, and perseverance, highlighting Forrest's impact on the world around him.

  4. harry porter :

    Harry Potter follows a young wizard, Harry, who discovers he's famous for surviving an attack by the dark wizard Voldemort. At Hogwarts, he learns magic, makes friends, and faces dangers as Voldemort returns to power. Over time, Harry confronts his past and ultimately defeats Voldemort to save the wizarding world.

  5. fluke :

    Fluke (1995) tells the story of a man who dies in an accident and is reincarnated as a dog. As a dog, he embarks on a journey to reconnect with his family and uncover the truth behind his past life. Along the way, he learns valuable lessons about love, loyalty, and second chances.
